Book Recommendations (Under Construction)

Disclaimer: these are academic book recommendations, and not books read for leisure (unless learning is leisure for you!). That said, here are some of my favorite books in each science (except chemistry because I've never got the hang of it).

Biology

Campbell Biology

You cannot be a biology olympiad aspirant without reading this book entirely. Campbell Biology, now in its 12th edition summarizes (with some decent depth) all fields of biology in one volume. It lays the foundation for further biological studies. It has numerous diagrams and easy-to-understand explanations. There is an emphasis on inferring the content from experiments. Would recommend this book for anyone looking for a solid base in biology

The biology books I've read are for olympiad purposes.
