Mutashabihat Guide

Welcome to mutashabihaat guide. This is an online, open source resource for aspiring huffadh to perfect their hifdh. Mutashabihat are the most challenging aspect of perfecting one’s memorization. A proficient hafidh must know each variation of a similar verse and which surah it is from and other relevant details. Every week, we will post one set of mutashabihaat and explain how I memorized it

قولوا ءامنا بالله vs قل ءامنا بالله

This set of mutashabihat is between Surah Baqarah and Surah Aal-’imran.
